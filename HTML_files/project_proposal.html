Project Proposal

by Ben Stork, Crisharon Beale, Robert Payne, Julie Pyle

Create a movie information data frame in SQL that allows users to quickly and conveniently search for the top movies based on IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes ratings.

Compare movie ratings on Rotten Tomatoes and IMDb to find the top 10 movies based on 8 major genres:
1. Action
2. Comedy
3. Documentary
4. Drama
5. Family
6. Horror
7. Sci-fi
8. Western

Lastly, inform the consumer where to stream a movie.
